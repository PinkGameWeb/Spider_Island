Overview
This is a 3rd person survival game where you are a survivor that somehow ended up on this mysterious island. You somehow seemed to have been washed up on the island and now you must find a way back home.
On the island there are 8 crates on each leg of the spider. Collect all 8 to be able to build an escape boat to escape the island. Around the island are various foods for survival along with mostly dangerous creatures... with a few expections. Each spider leg has a completely unique environment and no two areas are the same.
